Safranco

Creating a showcase website for a food wholesaler using the Prestashop CMS. The goal is to develop an online presence for the company Safranco, allowing them to highlight their products and services, improve brand visibility, and potentially attract new customers.

Main Features:

  • Visual design and branding: Ensuring the website aligns with Safranco’s brand identity and communicates professionalism.
  • Product Search & Filters:
  • Listing of all product brands.
  • Listing of products category.
  • About Us: Information about the company’s background.
  • Contact page: Providing users with a way to reach out for inquiries or orders.
  • Google Calendar for booking appointments.
  • Phone, Mail and social network integration.
  • Google reviews clients testimonials.
  • Responsive mobile design.

Technologies Used:

  • Content Management System: Prestashop 8.11 version.
