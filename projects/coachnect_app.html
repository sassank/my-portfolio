======= Coaching App

Coaching Application

The goal of this application is to connect certified sports coaches with individuals looking to improve their fitness or achieve personal training goals in France. The platform allows users to easily find and book coaching sessions with certified professionals, ensuring they receive personalized support and guidance throughout their fitness journey.

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced the application provides a range of coaches who specialize in different types of training, from strength and conditioning to sports-specific coaching.

The platform also fosters a supportive community with a communication system that allows members to chat directly with their coaches, track their progress, and receive notifications for upcoming sessions and updates.

Main Features:

  • Sign up as a certified coach or a member (individual looking for coaching)
  • Browse and book personalized training sessions with professional coaches
  • Real-time chat functionality to facilitate communication between the coach and the member
  • Notification system for reminders about upcoming training sessions, cancellations, or special announcements
  • User-friendly profile setup where coaches and members can manage their information
  • Session history and performance tracking tools to monitor progress over time
  • Coach availability calendar, so members can easily see when coaches are free for new bookings

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: Flutter (IOS and Android cross-platform development)
  • Backend: Firebase (for database management and real-time data synchronization)
