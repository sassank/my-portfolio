Calmedica

Creation of an audio transcription platform for patients for Calmedica medical company.

Calmedica is a French start-up founded in 2013 and specialized in healthcare pathway management.

Main Features:

  • Visualization and management of patient information and interactions
  • Import and playback of audio files
  • Display of transcribed files in a conversation format with different styles for assistant and patient messages
  • Notifications for successful or failed patient additions
  • Addition of a color badge representing the status of an interaction
  • Processing and recording of phone calls
  • Features for transcription, speaker identification, summary generation, and keyword extraction

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: TypeScript
  • Backend: Prisma (Database), Next.js (API), Twilio (communication) & Whisper (automatic speech recognition system by OpenAI).
